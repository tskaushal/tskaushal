I'm a penultimate-year **Electronics and Communication Engineering** student at **VIT Chennai**, working across two tracks that don't usually share a room: **VLSI / RTL hardware design** and **AI / software infrastructure**.

On the hardware side I design and verify processors and accelerators in Verilog. On the software side I build production-grade RAG pipelines, research tooling, and full-stack apps. I like the friction between the two — it keeps me honest about what's actually happening under the abstraction.

- 🔬 Currently interning at **Maven Silicon**, building a 3-stage pipelined **RV32I RISC-V processor** with UVM verification
- 🧠 Research intern at **NIT Calicut**, building a fully offline **RAG pipeline** (FastAPI, LlamaIndex, ChromaDB, Ollama) with blue-green AWS deployments
- ⚡ Built a **4×4 INT8 systolic array AI accelerator** in Verilog — AXI-lite interface, ping-pong buffering, synthesized via Yosys
- 🎯 Preparing for **GATE ECE** and **MS in VLSI/ECE** applications alongside placement-track DSA
